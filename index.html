Approach

We study businesses deeply
and act with patience.

Our investment process begins with a simple question: is this a great business? We seek companies with durable competitive advantages, exceptional management, and the capacity to compound capital over long periods. We hold few positions, each representing our highest conviction.

We are indifferent to benchmarks. Our alignment is entirely with our partners — we succeed when they succeed. This clarity of purpose drives every decision, from the businesses we study to the prices we are willing to pay.

01

Research first.

Every thesis is built from primary research — conversations with industry participants, rigorous financial analysis, and an honest assessment of competitive dynamics.

02

Long time horizon.

We think in years, not quarters. The best businesses compound quietly and relentlessly. Our job is to identify them early and let time do the work.

03

Partner alignment.

Our capital sits alongside yours. We have no incentive to be active for activity's sake. Every decision is evaluated against a simple standard: is this in our partners' interest?
